FEATURED PROJECTS

REFLECT

Few things tug at our heartstrings more than a photograph. During my time as a portrait and wedding photographer, I noticed the need for a hub conecting professional photographers with charitable causes in need of a well-trained eye for imagery. I designed Reflect to fulfill that need.

NOMCENTS

The nomadic nature of food truckery presents unique needs for vendors and their customers. Nomcents fills those needs with a way to search local food trucks, view menus and reviews, select and order food, and complete payment, all from their iPhone.

BLOCBOX

As a part of my Apprenticeship at Bloc, I designed Blocbox - a SaaS (Software as a Service) web application which allows people to collect information and store it in a single place. Create simple documents, store images, links to websites, and collaborate with others.
