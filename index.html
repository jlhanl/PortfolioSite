FEATURED PROJECTS

REFLECT

Few things tug at our heartstrings more than a photograph. During my time as a portrait and wedding photographer, I noticed the need for a hub conecting professional photographers with charitable causes in need of a well-trained eye for imagery. I designed Reflect to fulfill that need.

NOMCENTS

The food truck industry has grown exponentially in the past several years, and its mobile nature presents unique needs for vendors and their customers. Nomcents fills those needs with a way to search local food trucks, view menus and reviews, select and order food, and complete payment, all from their iPhone.

BLOCBOX

As a part of my Apprenticeship at Bloc, I designed Blocbox. Blocbox is a SaaS (Software as a Service) web application which allows people to collect information and store it in a single place. A person can create simple documents, store images, links to websites, and collaborate with others.
