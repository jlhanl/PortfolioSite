A FORGED PATH

Is it possible to be at odds with
yourself but still benefit from that struggle?

For my ninth birthday, my favorite gift was a Smithsonian Grow-Your-Own Crystals kit. One one hand, my inner chemist was super excited about the fact that I would be in possession of my very own beaker and petri dish.

On the other hand, to my inner artist, there really wasn't anything more beautiful than the glittering edges of a purple gem. Not only could I study and observe the chemical reaction that built the crystal, I actually grew the pretty rock myself!

Over time, this duality of interests presented two competing forces: artist vs. scientist. As I moved onto college and into a career, there was always pressure to choose: let creativity run unbridled as a true artist, or focus my analytical nature into a hard skill.

Naturally, I chose both.

Now, while my path has taken a number of detours, I am thankful for that ongoing tug-of-war and for the unique insights it gives me into multiple perspectives. As a UX Designer and Developer, I bring a natural tendency for empathy, along with an innate desire to hunt down solutions and a passionate need to create and craft lovely things.

Discovering a profession that allows my to work for me is a gift, and it's one I would love to share.
